Commission information

To order a commission, reach me at [email protected]!
Prices range from $50 for landscapes, to $80 for more complex images. I'm available to work on projects, especially if they're related to games!

I have experience working on illustrations (backgrounds, icons, props) for game projects, mobile apps, children's illustration books, book and album covers, concept art and wallpapers.

If we're working together, you can expect me to first draw you a sketch, then a color-sketch, and when everything is approved and I'm sure it's what you want, I'll make it a finished painting.

Payments are accepted via Paypal, and you can pay me after the image is done and you're satisfied with results.
